About

Avsar Academy is the first and one of leading learning institutions in Chhattisgarh and was established with an aim to develop as the final destination for all career initiation programmes. Avsar's unflinching reputation has been built, in large part, by its un-compromising standards and focus on the intellectual and personal development of the individual learner. We want people who question, challenge and think creatively, and those who are prepared to take on new concepts and ideas.

Avsar Academy is Institute preparing candidates for the Civil Services Examination at all the three levels â€“ Preliminary Test, Main Examination and Personality Test. The Institute was founded in 1999 by Shashank Sharma & Smita Sharma who had to his credit numerous academic distinctions. Since its inception the Institute has helped over 500 students to enter the Civil Services including I.A.S., I.F.S., I.P.S. other Central Services And State Services. Every year some of our students have secured positions amongst the successful candidates.

The teaching faculty of the Institute has been drawn from highly qualified and experienced teachers of the Central Universities and other reputed Institutes.We welcome students who are prepared to interface their experience and diverse backgrounds with our high-end educational programmes, for their transformation to a developed personality prepared to face not only competitive examinations but also succeed in modeling successfully their respective career paths. Mr. Shashank Sharma, Mrs. Smita sharma & Team have been the torch bearer of this institute.
